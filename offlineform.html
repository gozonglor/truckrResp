Sanitize My Hard Drives Using NAID Certified Processes - I require PCs for People, as a condition of NAID AAA Certification, to sanitize or physically destory all computer hard drives being recycled. Opt-Out of Hard Drive Sanitization Using NAID Certified Processes - I choose to utilize PCs for People non-certified data sanitization. Each drive will receive a one-pass wipe and have Windows reinstalled, but serial numbers are not logged.
